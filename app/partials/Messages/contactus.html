Step 1: Welcome Page

There are multiple ways to quickly find a product and adding it to the shopping cart. You can access the Categories from the text menu, click the image icons, or use the Product Search bar at the top of the screen. At any time on this site, you can hover over the shopping cart icon at the top-right of the screen to preview your cart, access the View Cart screen, or complete your order via the Checkout button.

Step 2: Product Navigation

Navigate to specific products by clicking a Category or by using the Product Search bar. Once on this screen, you can quickly add items to the cart by adding a quantity and then pressing the cart icon. Alternatively, you can click the image of the product to view the Product Detail page. See Step 3 below for more information on the detail page.

Step 3: Product Detail Page

The Product Detail page provides the high resolution Image, Description, Tags, and any associated Videos for the product. Click the product image at the top-left to zoom in to the product. Add a Quantity and press the Add To Cart button to continue.

Step 4: View Cart

This screen can be accessed by hovering over the Shopping Cart icon on the top-right of any page, and clicking the View Cart button. The View Cart page shows all items that are presently in your shopping cart. You can change the quantity on the left side of each product or click the red "minus" symbol at the top-right of the product.

The Cancel Order button will remove all items from the shopping cart. Save Order will save any edits to the product quantities. The green Check Out button will take you to the next screen to complete your order.

Step 5: Checkout Screen

The Checkout page can be accessed at any time by hovering over the Shopping Cart icon on the top-right of any page, and clicking the Checkout button. You will be prompted to fill the Order section of this page, which requires you to select a Reason to Order. The optional Comments box will provide additional information to the Verizon order approver. The Shipping section requires an address to be entered. Your settings may automatically populate the Shipping address, which can be edited or changed. The Approval section will show any items in the shopping cart that will require order approval.

Prior to submitting the order, green checkmarks must appear to the left of the Order and Shipping sections. Press the Submit Order button when ready to complete the order.
